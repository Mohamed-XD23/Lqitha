06 Brand Voice
01
Precise
Every word earns its place. No filler, no jargon. Information is structured, scannable, and immediately useful.
"Your item has been verified. Claim it now."
02
Authoritative
We speak with calm confidence. Lqitha doesn't suggest — it confirms. Grounded and in control, never arrogant.
"Ownership confirmed. Transfer initiated."
03
Minimal
Silence is part of the design. Remove the decorative, keep the meaningful. Less is always more.
"Lost. Found. Verified."
04
Human
Behind every lost item is a story. We are efficient — never cold. Professional, but never robotic.
"We know what this means to you."
07 Do's & Don'ts
✓ Do
Use Cormorant Garamond for all display and heading text
Maintain clear space around the logo at all times
Use Sahara Gold sparingly — primary actions and key labels only
Write in short, declarative, confident sentences
Keep generous whitespace — it signals confidence
Use italic Cormorant for editorial emphasis
Always show verification status prominently
✗ Don't
Add drop shadows, glows or gradients to the logo
Stretch, skew or rotate the mark in any way
Overuse gold — it loses its premium value immediately
Use casual, playful or passive language in UI copy
Mix more than 2 typefaces in a single layout
Use border radius over 8px on cards or components
Introduce colors outside the defined brand palette
